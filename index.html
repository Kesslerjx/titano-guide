Titano

Guide

These instructions are based around using MetaMask, PancakeSwap, and BNB to acquire Titano

Required

  • MetaMask
  • BNB or a way to purchase it

Setup

Add BSC to MetaMask

  1. Open MetaMask
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Go to Networks
  4. Press Add Network
    • Network Name: Smart Chain
    • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
    • ChainID: 56
    • Symbol: BNB
    • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com
  5. Press Save

Add Titano to MetaMask

  1. Visit CoinMarketCap
  2. Copy the contract
  3. Open MetaMask
    • Ensure you're in the Smart Chain wallet and not Ethereum
  4. Press Import Tokens
  5. Paste the contract in the Token Address field
    • Everything should autofill
  6. Press Import

Add Titano to PancakeSwap

  1. Go to Titano on PancakeSwap
  2. Press Trade
  3. Press I Understand then Import

Connect your wallet to PancakeSwap

  1. Go to PancakeSwap
  2. Press Connect Wallet
  3. Choose your wallet
    • If it gives you an error, press More and use WalletConnect

Connect your wallet to Titano

  1. Visit the Titano dashboard
  2. Press Connect Wallet
  3. Use QR code to connect with your wallet

Purchasing

Acquire BNB

  • Purchase on your preferred exchange or swap another coin for it

Send BNB to your wallet

  • Verify that it's in there before moving on

Swap BNB for Titano

  1. Go to PancakeSwap
  2. Press Trade
  3. Ensure BNB is in the top field
  4. Choose the amount of BNB that you want to swap
  5. Ensure Titano is in the bottom field
  6. Adjust Slippage Tolerance
    1. Press the settings button
    2. Set the Slippage Tolerance to 10%
      • Increase the Slippage Tolerance until the swap goes through
  7. Press Swap
    • It should open up your wallet and prompt you
    • If it doesn't, try closing your wallet and trying again
    • If you get a JSON error, up the Slippage Tolerance
