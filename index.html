Setup

Add BSC to MetaMask

  1. Open MetaMask
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Go to Networks
  4. Press Add Network
    • Network Name: Smart Chain
    • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
    • ChainID: 56
    • Symbol: BNB
    • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com
  5. Press Save

Add Titano to MetaMask

  1. Visit CoinMarketCap
  2. Copy the contract
  3. Open MetaMask
    • Ensure you're in the Smart Chain wallet and not Ethereum
  4. Press Import Tokens
  5. Paste the contract in the Token Address field
    • Everything should autofill
  6. Press Import

Connect your wallet to Titano

  1. Visit the Titano dashboard
  2. Press Connect Wallet
  3. Use QR code to connect with your wallet

Connect to BogSwap

  1. Visit the Titano dashboard
  2. Press Swap
    • It should take you to a page that's setup to swap BNB for Titano
  3. Press Connect Wallet
  4. Use WalletConnect to connect to your MetaMask wallet

Purchasing

Acquire BNB

  • Purchase on your preferred exchange or swap another coin for it
  • There will be a hold period if you purchase on Binance with USD

Send BNB to your wallet

  1. Open MetaMask
  2. Select the BNB token
  3. Select Receive
  4. Copy the address
    • Use this to send BNB from the exchange to your wallet
    • Verify that the BNB has been received before moving on

Swap BNB for Titano

  1. Go back to BogSwap
    • Since your wallet should be connect at this point, you should see your BNB balance
    • Ensure BNB and Titano are in the From and To field
  2. Choose the amount of BNB that you want to swap
  3. Leave slippage at 1%
  4. Press Swap
    • It should open up your wallet and prompt you to confirm
    • If it doesn't, try closing your wallet and trying again
    • If it fails or you get an error, up the Slippage
